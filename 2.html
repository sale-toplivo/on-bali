Where to stay in Bali: Best areas for travelers

Ubud : There's nothing like staying in the heart of the action, particularly when you've got awesome attractions like Ubud Monkey Forest within a short distance. After a long day of marveling at the sights, climb into a cozy bed at the Boutique Charming Villa Surrounded by Palm Trees , the SUARSENA BUNGALOW UBUD or the Aurora House . Kuta : If your time is limited here, make sure you see Kuta Beach. It's one of the many reasons so many tourists choose to travel to this destination. There are lots of comfortable places to stay here too, including The Pavilion Kuta , the 1 BR in Comfortable Atmosphere of a Boutique Resort in Kuta Beach - Bali and the Rita ZA Hotel . Denpasar : If you're new to this destination and have no idea where to hit the hay, we can help you out. The Inna Bali Heritage Hotel , the Capital O 1068 Grand Mirah Boutique and the Hotel Mawar 2 are terrific choices. All have excellent service and are close to iconic attractions such as Sanur Beach. Seminyak : For some of the best service in town, lock in a room at the One Eleven , the Uma Sapna or the Four On Drupadi . You'll be able to uncover more of this destination's biggest and best attractions with the cash you save, starting with Seminyak Square. Legian : Legian Beach is just one of the must-see attractions in this exciting part of Bali. Ensure you allow enough time to discover everything else this destination is known for. For places to lay your head, the Airy Legian 191 Kuta Bali , the Arca Bungalow and the Lumbung Sari Hotel should be on your shortlist.
